Are Freshers allowed to join?

Events like these aren't restricted to any specific set of people. Freshers can for sure join and come with their innovative submissions.

Do we need to pay anything?

Yes, You have to pay in terms of time you are putting in for this event. But in terms of money, 000000.0000 :)

I don't know how to design Websites, what to do?

WeDesign 2.0 is not a complicated event, it expects the participants to design a website using simple HTML, CSS, Javascript only. And Learning these aren't much difficult. Learning should never stop.

Can we register for multiple events?

Yes for sure, you can register for multiple events. If you can handle them properly, Go for it. More than winning any challenge, your main motive should be to come out with something mind blowing

Change details after filling form?

You can contact anyone from the Microsoft Innovations Club Team and the Team would guide you through the process - help you change the details.

Yes :) . For sure. Secrets aren't disclosed so early. Wait for the special announcements.
